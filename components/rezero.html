Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World

Introduction

Re:Zero - Starting Life in Another World (Re:Zero kara Hajimeru Isekai Seikatsu) is a popular Japanese light novel series adapted into an anime. It follows the story of Subaru Natsuki who is transported to a fantasy world and discovers he has the power to return from death.

Number of Releases

  • Season 1: 25 episodes (2016)
  • Season 2: 25 episodes (2020-2021)
  • OVA: Memory Snow (2018), The Frozen Bond (2019)

About

Re:Zero is known for its dark fantasy themes and emotional storytelling. It explores Subaru's struggles with despair and hope as he faces death repeatedly to protect his loved ones.

Author

Written by Tappei Nagatsuki, Re:Zero began as a web novel before being published as a light novel with illustrations by Shinichirou Otsuka.

Credits

  • Author: Tappei Nagatsuki
  • Illustrator: Shinichirou Otsuka
  • Anime Studio: White Fox
  • Director: Masaharu Watanabe
  • Music: Kenichiro Suehiro
